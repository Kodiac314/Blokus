# Blokus
Enjoy the classic 2..4 player board game. Take turns placing pieces to cover as much of the board as possible.

Click and drag pieces. Hover near the top or bottom of the piece list to scroll up/down.

<img width="586" height="474" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b30d6c0-2dad-4e1a-a4c6-5bb20c9a67de" />

Press 'r' to rotate, 'f' to flip, or WASD/Arrow keys to orient the selected piece
